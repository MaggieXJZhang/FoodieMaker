About Me

Xujia(Maggie) Zhang: 1B Biomedical Engineering Student

I am a foodie and I am a maker. Also, I cook delicious food so I have the magic power to make my guests foodies as well!

The foodie side of me started when I was very little. Refusing to do my homework, I found the kitchen fasinating. Looking at my grandparents prepping foods and turning raw ingredients into delicacies felt like magic. So I started helping as soon as I could. Rolling out dumpling wrappers with my mini rolling pin, making pizza with help from my mom, and helping out on prepping veggies, etc. As I grew older, I started trying out recipes I found online and spending all day testing recipes. Now, living on my own, and under the time pressure of university, I cook fast and deliciously. Wanna see what I eat? Check out this!

Am I a maker? Well, if not yet, I am in progress. Watching my grandpa repairing small objects and helping him has planted the seed of "maker" in me. I devoured the process of building and repairing. Thanks to the Gr. 8 Applied Skills class, I got better. From screwing back screws, to repairing hair clippers, to soldering inners of my handheld mixer, I have to say, my mom saved lots of money from buying new ones because of me.

To become better at the art of making, I am learning fast! From tech newbie to mastering SolidWorks, basic Arduino, Python, C#, and C++, I believe I will be a true maker soon! See my recent progress here!
