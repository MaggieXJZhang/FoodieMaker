My Yumminess

  • Steamed Red Bean Buns

    Feb. 13, 2018

    It was lunar Dec. 28. As a Chinese tradition, making steamed buns will bring you good fortune in the coming year. So I made cute bunny shaped red bean buns!


  • Garlic Okra

    Feb. 06, 2018

    Who won't want a new way of eating these slimy okras. After a quick bath in the boiling water, these babies are topped with sauted garlic and soya sauce. Even my roommate who hates vegetables liked it!


  • Pineapple Ninja

    Jan. 27, 2018

    After watching pineapples stalls years wondering how they've cut pineapples, I finally got a chance to do it myself. Holding a small knife, I diagnally cut along the stubs of pineapple and take out them. New skill acquired!
